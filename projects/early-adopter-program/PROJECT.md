# Early Adopter Program

## Goal

Sign 10 Early Adopters, onboard them successfully, and convert ≥7 to paying customers at the preferential rate of €39/month.

## Program Terms

- **Duration**: 3 months free
- **Spots**: 10 maximum
- **Price after 3 months**: €39/month (Starter plan public rate: €49/month) — lifetime
- **Onboarding**: 1 hour personalized call + data import
- **Support**: Prioritized WhatsApp + email
- **Access**: All features (Starter plan feature set)
- **Commitment from customer**: Use as primary tool for 3 months, 2 feedback calls, satisfaction survey, optional case study

## Qualification Criteria

- Independent kitchen business (dealer, manufacturer, installer) or small network (<5 locations)
- France or Belgium based
- Currently managing projects in Excel, paper, or generic CRM
- 10–150 projects/year
- Decision maker is signing up (owner or manager)

## Funnel Stages

| Stage | Owner | Trigger |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Identified | Sales Rep | Added to leads list |
| Contacted | Sales Rep | Outreach message sent |
| Interested | Sales Rep | Replied positively |
| EA Pitch Sent | Sales Rep | Early Adopter offer presented |
| Enrolled | Customer Success | Signed up + confirmed |
| Onboarded | Customer Success | Onboarding call completed |
| Month 1 Check | Customer Success | 30-day feedback call done |
| Month 3 Wrap | Customer Success | Conversion conversation done |
| Converted | CEO | Billing activated at €39/mo |

## Success Criteria

- 10 enrolled Early Adopters
- ≥7 converted to paid at end of 3 months
- ≥3 case studies published
- ≥5 NPS responses with average ≥8
- ≥2 referrals generated from EA cohort
