# 16-Week Go-To-Market Campaign

## Goal

**10 paying customers by 2026-08-01**, generating €490–€990 MRR.

## Phase Breakdown

### Phase 1: Validation (Weeks 1–4)
- 5 discovery interviews with kitchen business owners
- Early Adopter Program launched and announced
- LinkedIn company page live with first 3 posts
- Pitch deck + templates finalized

### Phase 2: Outbound (Weeks 5–8)
- 50 qualified prospects identified and messaged
- 3-touch LinkedIn + cold email sequences running
- 10 demos completed
- 3 Early Adopter enrollments

### Phase 3: Content & Social Proof (Weeks 9–12)
- 1 case study published
- 4 blog posts live (including comparison post vs. Winner Flex)
- 2-minute demo video (French) recorded and shared
- LinkedIn: 2 posts/week cadence established
- Houzz Pro profile live

### Phase 4: Network & Scale (Weeks 13–16)
- SNEC partnership conversation initiated
- EuroCucina 2026 attendance decision made (April 21–26 Milan — if ROI positive)
- Referral program activated (once 3 customers signed)
- 10 paying customers achieved

## Success Criteria

- 10 paying customers (any plan)
- MRR ≥ €490
- ≥20 demos completed
- ≥3 Early Adopters converted to paid
- ≥1 published case study with NPS ≥ 8

## Budget

Total: <€2,000
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator: €100/month × 2 = €200
- Cuisines & Bains Magazine ad: €500–1,500 (Phase 3 only, if ROI justified)
- Misc (printing, events): €200
