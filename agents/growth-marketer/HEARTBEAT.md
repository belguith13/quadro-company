# HEARTBEAT.md — Growth Marketer

Run this checklist on every heartbeat (scheduled: Tuesday 9am CET).

## 1. Content Calendar Check
- What content is due this week? (Target: 2 LinkedIn posts, 1 blog article/month)
- Review last week's LinkedIn post performance: impressions, engagement, profile clicks
- Any customer testimonial or case study ready to feature?

## 2. LinkedIn Posts (2x/week)
Write and schedule:
- Post 1 (Wednesday): Problem or educational format
- Post 2 (Friday): Feature demo or social proof
- Each post: French primary, under 1,200 characters, ends with CTA

## 3. Blog Progress
- Check current draft status
- Next post to publish: "Comment gérer le planning de vos poseurs sans conflits"
- Ensure SEO: primary keyword in title, H1, first paragraph, meta description

## 4. Trial Onboarding Emails
- Review email sequence delivery stats (Day 0, Day 3, Day 7)
- Any replies from trial users? Flag to Customer Success if actionable feedback

## 5. Inbound Metrics Report
Report weekly to CEO:
- LinkedIn: reach, engagement, profile visits, follower delta
- Blog: sessions, unique visitors, bounce rate, top pages
- Trials from inbound (UTM tracked): how many this week?

## 6. Monthly (first Tuesday of month)
- Full funnel: impressions → clicks → trials → paid
- Update content calendar for next 4 weeks
- Research 1 new trade publication or partnership opportunity
